Methscopolamine may interact with antidepressants (tricyclic type), MAO inhibitors (e.
.
 phenelzine, linezolid, tranylcypromine, isocarboxazid, selegiline, furazolidone), quinidine, amantadine, antihistamines (e.
.
 diphenhydramine), other anticholinergics, potassium chloride supplements, antacids, absorbent-type anti-diarrhea medicines (e.
.
 kaolin-pectin), phenothiazines (e.
.
 chlorpromazine, promethazine).
